# Zorblax 3000 — Owner's Manual (sample)

The **Zorblax 3000** is a fictional espresso machine used here as demo data for
the RAG knowledge base.

## Specifications
- Brew time: **7 seconds** per cup.
- Water tank capacity: **2 liters**.
- Warranty: **5 years** parts and labour.
- Power: 1450 W, 230 V.

## Care
Descale the Zorblax 3000 every **60 brews** using citric-acid solution. Do not
immerse the base in water. Replace the gasket annually.

## Support
For the fictional support line, dial 1-800-ZORBLAX. Warranty claims require the
serial number printed under the drip tray.
